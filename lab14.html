Principal's message

As the Principal of the college, I seek to foster multi-stakeholder partnerships and also have a sustained engagement with them. The main stakeholders of institutions of higher learning are the students, who are in their formative years. I aim to create a conducive environment for them so that they are emboldened to embark on their new journey with confidence, competence and caliber and be successful in the present times marked by unprecedented changes. I welcome all the students entering into the portals of the college to be a part of the invigorating and energizing experience offered here. Here, they have the opportunities to develop their innate capabilities under the guidance and mentoring of proficient faculty and dedicated support staff.
Dr. Harpreet Kaur
Principal
Mata Sundri College for Women
University of Delhi
